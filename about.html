About Us





Many heinous crimes were catching the news in the past few days. Society was not aware of all these because they would not have brought TRP for the news channels. The media was not ready to show the cases but whosoever read them from anywhere became angry. They were not ready to see the society heading this way but no one was coming forward to change it. We could not have seen any more of it. Coming up with an idea of changing society by using youth power, we decided to kick off SIBSS, and here we are in front of you. Making society a better place to live.


Changing the mindset of the youth, guiding them, motivating them to work for the nation. Instead of finding opportunities in foreign lands and serving them, we should focus on building new opportunities in INDIA. Making the youth aware of the importance of life, telling them what they have to do with their life is our main aim. Most lives in the world begin, work day and night, end but we inspire youth to add more and more chapters in their life.


So that when the inevitable death approaches them, instead of crying, they can be happy, they can adjust their mustache proudly and say that they have done something in life and die with a smile on their face. That's the spirit that we want to awaken in each and every mind of India. We begin with INDIA and stop not until INDIA is the INDIA we aim for. Instead of going to foreign to work, we should make India such a great place that foreigners are willing to work for us.


The day when a small child will go out alone, without any fear will be the day of our success. The day when the female of our society will not fear to go out at 1 AM alone, will be the day of our success. The day when the youth of the society will not complain about the very less number of opportunities available will be the day of our success!!! The day when people will prefer INDIA over western countries will be a day of our success.


And of course, all this is not possible without your support. So please support us so that we, our children, our loved ones are living in a society where we are not afraid of anything! Even the smallest of the smallest support whether it is in form of donation(contact us to donate), arranging webinars so that we are reaching new people(contact us), joining us, etc. would be of great help.


Without you we are nothing, without you country is nothing, so let us together come for the country and show the world what we are capable of!!!





Written by Ayush Gupta
