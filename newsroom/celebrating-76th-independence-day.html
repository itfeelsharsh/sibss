Celebrating 76th Independence Day

AND here we are, celebrating the 76th Independence Day, the most auspicious day for everybody associated with India. Our fighters then, sacrificed their precious existences for the nation and our fighters now are trying their best to maintain what they've left for us. Let's owe this liberty to those who helped us define who we are, guide what we do, remind us of the work that history has given us in our own time.


Today, we honor the memory of those who boldly fought for the Indian independence, the REAL leaders and the people.


The ethos of our freedom struggle forms the foundation of modern India. Youth is required to stop complaining and start working for the amelioration. Some of the issue is, us, the people of nation. Independence day had given us a new start long ago, then why are we still waiting for the right things to happen? Why are we ranting about the wrong? Why are we asking if we failed as a democracy? without even trying for once? We are here to change and we shall do it.


Nevertheless, huge thanks to those who continue to show the bravery and selflessness required to keep us that way.



From ~ Pallavi
