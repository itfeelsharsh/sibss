What is SIBSS?

Serve India Before Serving Self

SIBSS, Serve India Before Serving Self, welcomes you to the organization. This is an organization for youth, of youth, and by youth who are stepping ahead to bring change in the country making the society a safer, better, open-minded, and liberal place to live in.SIBSS is an initiative that aims towards a better society. Changing the mindset of the youth of the nation.
