Discord Server Rules [ALL and in depth]

1. Be respectful, civil, and welcoming.

Sometimes discussion can get heated, but you are responsible for your own behavior. This includes not targeting individuals and not posting personal information.



2. No NSFW content

This is including chat, avatars, pictures, memes, artworks, and nicknames. -If found to be sending NSFW content, it will result in instant removal. (BAN)



3. No any kind of discriminatory speech.

We do not welcome these types of speech.



4. No promotions

Do not advertise streams, YouTube channels, Discord servers, Reddit posts, and referral links. Unsolicited advertisements sent to members via private messages will result in instant removal (ban)



5. Do not post sensitive information such as a dox

We respect everyone's identity and existence. No fishing for people's private information by posting DOX.



6. Respect Everyone's Privacy

Being part of this group requires mutual trust. Authentic, expressive discussions make groups great, but may also be sensitive and private. What's shared in the group should stay in the group.
